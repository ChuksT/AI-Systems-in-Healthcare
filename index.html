From Prototype to Practice

Implementing Trustworthy Human–AI Systems in Healthcare

Presented at the 24th International Conference on Artificial Intelligence in Medicine (AIME 2026), Ottawa, Canada

Workshop Overview

Health systems around the world are rapidly moving from experimenting with AI to deploying it in real clinical and operational settings. Yet turning a promising prototype into a trusted, sustainable, and human-centered solution remains one of the hardest challenges in healthcare AI.

This highly interactive workshop is designed for researchers, clinicians, implementers, and health system leaders who want to bridge that gap.

  • Hands-on data workshop
  • Privacy, legal, and governance considerations
  • Codesign and multidisciplinary collaboration
  • Real-world implementation guidance
  • Collaborative problem-solving sessions
